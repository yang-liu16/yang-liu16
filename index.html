Career Profile

Yang Liu is pursuing a doctorate degree in Mathematics under the supervision of Prof. Zongxia Liang. He is currently visiting Prof. Ruodu Wang at University of Waterloo, Canada (October 2019 - September 2020). He received his bachelor degree in Mathematics (2016) at Tsinghua University.

Yang Liu's research area covers mathematical finance, actuarial science, stochastic control and optimization.
